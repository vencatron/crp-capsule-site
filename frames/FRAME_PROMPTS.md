# Frame Generation Prompts for Real Estate Capsule

This document contains the detailed prompts to generate the scroll-driven animation frames using Gemini/Nano Banana.

## Visual Style Guide
*   **Palette:** Deep Navy Blue (#1a365d), Gold/Bronze (#b8860b), White.
*   **Aesthetic:** Corporate, clean, flat design, modern, trustworthy.
*   **Format:** 16:9 Aspect Ratio.

---

## Act 1: The Problem (Frames 1-25)

**Sequence Goal:** Text fade-in and Pie Chart animation.

*   **Frame 0001-0010 Prompt:**
    > Corporate presentation background. Solid deep navy blue (#1a365d). Clean, bold white text in the center: "Stop Paying Taxes." Text opacity starts low (10%) and increases to 100%. Minimalist style.

*   **Frame 0011-0020 Prompt:**
    > Corporate motion graphics. Deep navy blue background. A 2D flat-style pie chart appears. The chart is mostly white (Net Income) with a large red slice (40%) representing "Taxes". Text label "40% Taxes" appears next to the red slice. Clean vector art style.

*   **Frame 0021-0025 Prompt:**
    > Deep navy blue background. Center text changes to a provocative question: "What if that capital could build wealth instead?" Font is modern sans-serif, white, high contrast.

## Act 2: The Solution (Frames 26-50)

**Sequence Goal:** Transformation of money into assets.

*   **Frame 0026-0035 Prompt:**
    > Concept art of financial transformation. A glowing gold dollar sign ($) in the center morphs into a stylized commercial building icon. Deep navy background. The transition should look smooth and liquid.

*   **Frame 0036-0050 Prompt:**
    > Corporate animation frame. A stylized icon of a commercial building sits on a gold line. Below it, text appears: "We Don't Flip Houses. We Build Oil Wells." The text "Oil Wells" is in gold color.

## Act 3: How It Works (Frames 51-80)

**Sequence Goal:** Cost Segregation visual explanation.

*   **Frame 0051-0060 Prompt:**
    > Financial infographic. A photo-realistic or high-quality vector illustration of an apartment complex. A price tag label attached to it reads "$1,200,000". Navy blue background.

*   **Frame 0061-0070 Prompt:**
    > Cost segregation diagram. The building is visually split into layers. Bottom layer (Land) is grayed out. Upper layers (Building) are highlighted. A magnifying glass icon hovers over the building details (fixtures, flooring).

*   **Frame 0071-0080 Prompt:**
    > Tax benefit highlight. A large gold arrow points down from the building to a tax document icon. Text overlay: "$300,000 Year 1 Deduction" and "100% Bonus Depreciation".

## Act 4: Partnership Structure (Frames 81-105)

**Sequence Goal:** LLC and Protection layers.

*   **Frame 0081-0090 Prompt:**
    > Network diagram. Three circular profile icons (representing partners) are arranged at the top. Lines connect them down to a central box labeled "LLC". Gold and white lines on navy background.

*   **Frame 0091-0105 Prompt:**
    > Risk protection visualization. The "LLC" box is surrounded by multiple shield outlines appearing one by one. Labels on shields: "LLC Status", "Insurance", "D&O Coverage".

## Act 5: The Financial Cycle (Frames 106-130)

**Sequence Goal:** Cash flow loop.

*   **Frame 0106-0130 Prompt:**
    > Circular flow chart. Icons arranged in a circle: Money Bag -> Building -> Graph Rising -> Piggy Bank. Arrows connect them in a clockwise loop. The arrows glow to show movement/flow of capital.

## Act 6: Call to Action (Frames 131-150)

**Sequence Goal:** Final CTA.

*   **Frame 0131-0145 Prompt:**
    > Process steps. Three numbered items appear sequentially: "1. Intro Call", "2. Strategy Session", "3. Deal Review". White text, left-aligned, gold numbers.

*   **Frame 0146-0150 Prompt:**
    > Final screen. Company logo (placeholder shield icon) in center. Large text: "Start Building Today." Button-like shape below with text "Schedule Consultation".
